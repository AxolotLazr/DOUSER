What DOUSER is

DOUSER is a Roguelite Boomer-Shooter, think of games such as Doom (1993) and The Binding of Isaac.

The gameplay of DOUSER

The gameplay of DOUSER is intended to be fast paced and challenging, reminiscent of games such as Ultrakill and Risk of Rain 2, encountering more and more enemies as your playthrough goes on.

The story of DOUSER

Premises:

  • Salfroom Industries took over and destroyed competitive water companies, limiting the access to it and hiking up the price exponentially.
  • The protagonist, Judge Vic Cherry, set a personal goal to make water accessable to anyone without having to pay enormous prices.
